Venues



As well as delivering teaching within your own home, I teach classes at five different venues. You can see information on them below.

  • Grewelthorpe Village Hall.
  • Kirkby Malzeard Mechanics Institute Village Hall.
  • Littlethorpe Village Hall.
  • The Karate Dojo.
  • Upstage Academy.

Classes are held on Mondays, 0930-1230.

A friendly and welcoming class with a mixture of abilities and ages. Only a mile from Ripon town centre and close to Littlethorpe church, the village hall has plenty of free parking.

The Karate Dojo
1-3 Queen Street
Town Centre
Ripon
HG4 1EG

Just off the market square above Yorkshire Trading Stores. You will find the entrance at the rear of the building near the bus station.

Google Maps Link.

One of the best venues in the area. This is a high quality, purpose-built room with soft flooring and mirrors. There is plenty of parking either in the Market Square or in Sainsbury's car park, where there is no charge after 1800.

This is a mixed ability class and we welcome beginners, improvers and intermediate participants.

The classes are held on Mondays, 1345-1445.
