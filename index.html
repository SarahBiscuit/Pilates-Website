Pilates is a type of mind-body exercise. Mind-body provide benefits on both a physical and a mental level. They use repetitive exercises to target muscles in the body

Some of the benefits of pilates include: -

  • Flatter stomach
    •
  • Stronger back
    •
  • Increased flexibility
    •
  • Excellent full body toning
    •
  • Improved core stability
    •
  • Relax and refresh
    •

Matwork Pilates refers to practicing pilates on a mat without a need for any other specialised equipment.

Pilates is an excellent way to improve strength, flexibility and muscle tone. It: -

  • Teaches body awareness and controlled movement.
    •
  • Promotes good posture and flexibility.
    •
  • Incorporates weight-bearing and resistance training.
    •
  • Improves strength in your core trunk muscles.
    •
  • Strengthens internal muscles while toning the visible ones.
    •

Pilates can be adapted for all fitness levels, and done with or without weights. It can help you increase your strength, balance, stability, core strength and flexibility. It also has significant medical benefits.

There are a number of medical benefits to pilates including: -

  • The weight-bearing and resistance training incorporated in pilates is highly recommended by physicians.
    •
  • Strengthening the core muscles can help alleviate back problems.
